Introduction
Society members have been collecting their family data for many years. These generous men and women provided the basis for this database.

Except where indicated in notes, birth, marriage, death, and burial data come from secondary sources. Census, military, and immigration data come from online databases containing original images. Obituaries are transcribed from newspaper databases and other online sources.

If you wish to correct data presented here or donate your family data, please contact the society using the information listed below.
